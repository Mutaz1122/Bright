About Bright


Bright is a speciality coffee with verity of top shelf beans and exclusive, home made desserts. Bright is a drive-through coffee with a unique design and colorful packaging. Bright is a coffee shop from different prospective. The industrial model of Bright is considered to be registered with Saudi Authority For Intellectual Property. In addition, Bright is a trade mark registered with Saudi Authority For Intellectual Property and recognized in GCC as a trade mark.
